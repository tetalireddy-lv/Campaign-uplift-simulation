# KPI & Measurement Rules

## KPI Framework

### Primary KPI Requirements
- Must be directly tied to business objective
- Must have a defined baseline (current performance)
- Must have a specific target (expected outcome)
- Must have a measurement window (start/end)
- Must have a defined data source

### Common Campaign KPIs by Objective

| Objective | Primary KPI | Supporting KPIs |
|-----------|------------|----------------|
| Lead Generation | MQLs generated | CPL, conversion rate, pipeline value |
| Conversion | Conversion rate | Revenue, AOV, CAC |
| Retention | Churn reduction | NPS, engagement rate, LTV |
| Awareness | Reach / Impressions | Brand recall, SOV, engagement |
| Upsell/Cross-sell | Revenue per customer | Attach rate, basket size |
| Reactivation | Reactivated accounts | Win-back rate, reactivation revenue |
| App Adoption | DAU/MAU | Feature adoption rate, session length |

## Measurement Plan Requirements

1. **Baseline** — What is the current performance?
2. **Target** — What is the expected improvement?
3. **Window** — Over what period is the target measured?
4. **Attribution** — How do we attribute results to the campaign?
5. **Reporting** — How often and to whom?
6. **Data Source** — Where does the data come from?

## Attribution Models

| Model | When to Use |
|-------|------------|
| Last-touch | Simple, single-channel campaigns |
| First-touch | Awareness-focused campaigns |
| Multi-touch | Multi-channel, long-cycle campaigns |
| Time-decay | Campaigns with clear consideration phase |
| Algorithmic | High-budget, data-rich environments |

## KPI Quality Checks

- KPI must be SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
- Target must be realistic given budget, timeline, and audience size
- Baseline must be from a comparable period
- Secondary KPIs should not conflict with primary
- Vanity metrics alone are insufficient (impressions without engagement)

## Red Flags

- No baseline defined
- Target is "increase" without a number
- Measurement window not specified
- No attribution model for multi-channel
- KPI not connected to business objective
- Reporting cadence not defined
