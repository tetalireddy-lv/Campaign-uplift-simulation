# Compliance & Brand Review Rules

## Brand Guidelines

### Tone and Voice
- All campaign messaging must align with brand tone (professional, approachable, authoritative)
- Avoid superlatives without substantiation ("best", "fastest", "only")
- Do not make comparative claims without evidence

### Visual Standards
- Brand colors, typography, and logo usage must follow brand book
- Photography and imagery must meet diversity and inclusion standards
- All assets must pass accessibility checks (contrast, alt text, readability)

## Compliance Rules

### Claims Validation
- All performance claims must be substantiated with data or citations
- Financial projections require disclaimers
- Healthcare claims require regulatory review
- Testimonials require consent documentation

### Data and Privacy
- Email campaigns must comply with CAN-SPAM / GDPR / local regulations
- SMS requires explicit opt-in documentation
- Retargeting requires cookie consent
- Audience data usage must comply with privacy policy

### Disclosure Requirements
- Sponsored content must be clearly labeled
- Affiliate relationships must be disclosed
- Contest/promotion rules must be included
- Terms and conditions must be accessible

### Industry-Specific Rules
- Financial services: fair lending, APR disclosures
- Healthcare: HIPAA considerations, no diagnostic claims
- Education: outcomes substantiation
- Insurance: state-specific requirements

## Risk Levels

| Risk Level | Description | Action Required |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| Critical | Regulatory violation, legal exposure | Must resolve before launch |
| High | Brand misalignment, unsubstantiated claims | Requires manager + legal review |
| Medium | Best practice deviation, minor guideline issue | Flag for manager awareness |
| Low | Style preference, optional improvement | Note in report |

## Compliance Checklist

- [ ] All claims substantiated
- [ ] Privacy requirements met for all channels
- [ ] Disclosures included where required
- [ ] Brand guidelines followed
- [ ] Accessibility standards met
- [ ] Legal review completed (if required)
- [ ] Consent documentation in place
