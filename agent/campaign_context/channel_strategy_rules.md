# Channel Strategy Rules

## Channel Selection Principles

1. **Audience-first** — Choose channels where the target audience is active
2. **Objective-aligned** — Channel role must support the campaign objective
3. **Budget-realistic** — Channel cost must fit within allocated budget
4. **Measurable** — Channel must support the required KPI measurement
5. **Timing-appropriate** — Channel lead time must fit timeline

## Channel Roles

| Channel | Best For | Typical Role |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Email | Nurture, conversion, retention | Direct response, personalized CTA |
| Paid Search | Intent capture, conversion | Bottom-funnel demand capture |
| Paid Social | Awareness, consideration, retargeting | Mid-funnel engagement |
| Display | Awareness, retargeting | Top-funnel reach |
| Content/SEO | Education, trust-building | Long-term organic acquisition |
| Events/Webinars | Engagement, qualification | Mid-funnel pipeline acceleration |
| Direct Mail | High-value accounts, reactivation | Personalized ABM outreach |
| In-App | Adoption, upsell, retention | Contextual engagement |
| SMS | Urgency, reminders, time-sensitive | Transactional or promotional nudge |
| PR/Earned Media | Awareness, credibility | Brand authority building |

## Channel Combination Rules

- **Conversion campaigns** should include at least one direct response channel (email, paid search)
- **Awareness campaigns** should include at least one reach channel (display, paid social, PR)
- **Multi-channel campaigns** should define a channel hierarchy (primary, supporting, amplification)
- **Retargeting** should be planned if the funnel has multiple stages

## Channel Budget Allocation Heuristics

- Do not allocate more than 60% of budget to a single channel unless justified
- Reserve 10-15% for testing and optimization
- Allocate measurement/analytics costs separately

## Channel Consistency Requirements

- Messaging must be adapted per channel but consistent in theme
- CTA must be appropriate to channel context
- Visual identity must follow brand guidelines across all channels
- Frequency capping should be considered for multi-channel campaigns
